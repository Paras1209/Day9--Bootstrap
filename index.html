How to Survive Your First Zombie Encounter

When you first encounter a zombie, your survival depends on staying calm and following these critical steps:

  1. Stay Quiet: Zombies are attracted to noise. Move silently and avoid running unless absolutely necessary.
  2. Assess the Threat: Count the number of zombies and identify escape routes.
  3. Aim for the Head: If combat is unavoidable, target the brain to neutralize the threat.
  4. Conserve Energy: Don't waste stamina on unnecessary movements.

Remember: Your goal is survival, not heroics. Live to fight another day.
